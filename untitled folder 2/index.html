AI & Career Automation

Hypothesis

The characteristics of a professional career—including required skills, industry sector, and workplace environment—directly influence the level of risk in job automation and replacement by AI services.

Methodology

This analysis examines 500 job positions across 10 career categories, evaluating automation risk through the lens of three key factors: skill requirements, industry trends, and workplace flexibility. Each career is analyzed to provide insights into market stability and future projections.
